Our Story

About Apex Motors

Who We Are

Passion Drives
Everything We Do

Founded in Beverly Hills in 2004, Apex Motors was born from a singular conviction: that acquiring a luxury automobile should be as extraordinary as driving one.

We are more than a dealership. We are automotive connoisseurs — a team of enthusiasts who live and breathe performance, craftsmanship, and design. Our showroom is a curated sanctuary for those who demand the finest machines in the world.

Over two decades, we have helped thousands of discerning clients find their perfect vehicle, built lasting relationships across six continents, and cemented our reputation as one of the world's most trusted purveyors of premium and exotic automobiles.

Our Journey

Two Decades of Excellence

2004

The Beginning

Nathaniel Apex opens a small showroom on Rodeo Drive with 12 hand-selected vehicles and a vision to revolutionize the luxury automotive retail experience.

2008

Global Expansion

Despite the global financial crisis, Apex Motors thrives by doubling down on client relationships. Opens a second location in Miami and begins sourcing vehicles internationally.

2012

Partnerships & Prestige

Secures official partnerships with Ferrari, Lamborghini, and Bentley. Apex becomes the exclusive West Coast dealer for three limited-run hypercar series.

2017

The Digital Pivot

Launches a new digital showroom experience, allowing clients to virtually configure and preview vehicles from anywhere in the world. First dealership to offer white-glove nationwide delivery.

2021

Electric Horizons

Opens a dedicated Electric Vehicle wing, curating the finest in sustainable performance. Partners with Rimac and Lucid Motors as their premium retail partner.

2024

A New Chapter

Celebrates 20 years of excellence with a complete showroom renovation, a private client club launch, and the opening of the Apex Motors Foundation — supporting emerging automotive designers worldwide.
